        Educational Section

        • What makes a strong password? Longer length + unique characters + resistance to patterns.
        • What is entropy? A rough measure of unpredictability (higher is harder to guess).
        • What is brute force? Trying many guesses until the correct password is found.
        • Why password reuse is dangerous? If one site is breached, attackers may try the same password elsewhere.
